Common questions
Things founders ask before signing.

Straight answers. No fluff.

If I'm not in support, how do I know what's happening?
Every ticket is tagged by category, topic, and sentiment. Bug patterns, feature requests, and confusion clusters surface automatically. You get a clear picture of what customers are struggling with — without having to live in the queue to see it.
How do I know tickets are being handled fast enough?
Response times and resolution rates are tracked inside the helpdesk continuously. You'll always know where volume is spiking and which ticket types are taking longest — not because you're watching, but because the data is reported to you.
What if your team misses a cancellation?
Every cancel intent gets a structured save attempt — a pause offer, a downgrade path, or a direct conversation — before the account closes. Nothing slips through because the process doesn't rely on someone noticing. It's built into how every cancellation is handled.
How do I know the retention process is actually working?
Every save attempt is logged — the reason for cancellation, what was offered, and whether the account was retained. That data accumulates over time so you can see which products, price points, and customer segments are churning most, and why.
What if my helpdesk isn't set up properly?
We assess your current setup on the first call. If it can support automation and tagging, we configure it. If it can't, we recommend the right tool and handle the migration as part of month one. You don't need to figure that out before talking to us.
What happens if we need more capacity?
The pod scales with your volume. Additional agents can be added at any time as your ticket load grows. We build the system in month one so that adding capacity is straightforward — not a re-architecture every time you grow.
